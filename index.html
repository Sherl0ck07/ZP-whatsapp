About Zilla Parishad Buldhana

Zilla Parishad Buldhana is the governing local body responsible for planning, development, and welfare activities in Buldhana District, Maharashtra. As a pivotal institution in rural governance, we are committed to the holistic development of our district through transparent administration and citizen-centric services.

The General Administration Department oversees operations, human resource management, public services, and digital governance under the "DigiSeva" initiative. DigiSeva represents our commitment to leveraging technology for improved service delivery, transparency, and accessibility for all citizens of Buldhana District.

Our Key Departments

Rural Development and Panchayat Raj

Health and Family Welfare

Provides healthcare services and promotes public health initiatives across the district.

Digital Initiatives under DigiSeva

Online Grievance Redressal

Citizens can submit issues online and receive updates via SMS for transparent complaint resolution.

e-Governance Dashboard

Real-time data visualization for rural development schemes and progress tracking.

Smart Attendance System

Automated attendance tracking for ZP officers ensuring accountability and efficiency.

ZP Connect App

Digital communication platform connecting officers and citizens for seamless interaction.

Digital Records Management

Paperless file movement and approval system for faster administrative processes.

Rural e-Library Program

Promoting digital learning and knowledge access in villages across Buldhana District.

Farmers Support Portal

Agriculture updates, weather alerts, and government schemes information for farmers.
