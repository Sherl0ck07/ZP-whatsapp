rivacy Policy for #ZP Buldhana WhatsApp Bot

Effective Date: 29th September 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the #ZP Buldhana WhatsApp Bot (“Bot”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information while interacting with the Bot. By using the Bot, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

The Bot may collect the following information:

User Inputs: Messages, menu selections, and interactions you provide.

Contact Details: Phone number (from WhatsApp) to personalize your interaction.

Service Usage Data: Logs of your interactions, such as queries made and options selected.

3. Use of Information

Collected information is used to:

Provide and improve the services of the Bot.

Respond to your queries and service requests efficiently.

Personalize your experience based on language and preferences.

Send follow-ups for inactive sessions (after 1 hour of inactivity).

4. Information Sharing

We respect your privacy and do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

When required by law or governmental authorities.

To comply with legal obligations or protect rights, property, or safety.

5. Data Retention

Your data will be retained only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations.

Inactive sessions and user inputs may be automatically deleted after a set period.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption of messages and secure storage. However, no method is 100% secure, and users should interact with the Bot responsibly.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Bot is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users will be notified through the Bot about significant changes.
